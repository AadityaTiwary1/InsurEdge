Project Summary

A short overview of what InsurEdge solves and why it matters.

InsurEdge was built to solve a very practical problem: gig workers often lose income due to factors that are completely outside their control. Heavy rainfall, extreme traffic, pollution, and area restrictions can reduce their working hours and earnings instantly. This project introduces an AI-powered parametric insurance approach where machine learning models help predict disruption risk, personalize weekly premiums, evaluate rider trustworthiness, and support automated, fraud-resistant payout decisions. Instead of treating insurance as a manual process, InsurEdge reimagines it as a smarter, faster, and more adaptive protection system.

About InsurEdge

Why the project was built and what makes it different.

Why It Was Built

InsurEdge was inspired by the everyday uncertainty faced by gig workers. Delivery riders often lose income because of external disruptions, yet they usually have no structured financial protection. This project was created to explore how AI and insurance logic can be combined to build something more practical and fair.

What Makes It Unique

The project goes beyond a single prediction model. It combines a Risk Score Model, Premium Prediction Model, Trust Score Model, and fraud-aware decision logic to create a connected insurance intelligence system rather than a basic ML demo.

Core Vision

At its heart, InsurEdge focuses on protecting income instead of only insuring physical assets. It is built around the idea that vulnerable workers should have access to fast, data-driven, and fair financial protection.
